Every Dataset Tells a Story — Here’s Mine.

From my first dataset in 2018 to designing impactful analytics solutions in 2025, this is my journey as a Data Analyst.

My journey into data analytics began in high school when I first explored spreadsheets to make sense of student performance data. That curiosity deepened during my B.Tech in Computer Science (2019–2023), where I learned Python, SQL, and data visualization. I realized data wasn’t just numbers — it was a way to uncover hidden patterns and drive real-world decisions.

Today, as a Data Analyst Trainee at Naresh i Technologies, I work on building data pipelines, optimizing SQL queries, and designing dashboards that turn raw datasets into clear business insights. My focus is on precision, speed, and storytelling — whether it’s analyzing 500K+ retail records in Power BI or developing predictive models for healthcare data.
