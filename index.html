About Me

I’m a Data Analyst with a strong foundation in SQL, Python, and Business Intelligence tools. I specialize in transforming raw datasets into insights and strategies that organizations can act on.

My background includes building ETL pipelines, creating interactive dashboards in Power BI & Tableau, and applying statistical analysis to uncover hidden patterns. During my internships and projects, I worked on real-world datasets — from analyzing city air quality to forecasting retail sales — always with the goal of turning data into clear business outcomes.

I hold a B.Tech in Computer Software Engineering, where I developed not just technical expertise but also the problem-solving mindset to approach data challenges creatively.

Looking ahead, I’m driven to help organizations make forward-looking, data-informed decisions — empowering them to not only solve today’s problems but also anticipate future opportunities.
