I transform raw datasets into clear, actionable insights that help organizations make data-driven decisions and anticipate future opportunities.

My journey into data began with a fascination for finding patterns in complexity. This curiosity led me to pursue a B.Tech in Computer Science, where I discovered a passion for transforming raw numbers into compelling stories that drive strategic decisions.

Today, as a Data Analyst, I specialize in developing end-to-end data solutions. I engineer robust ETL pipelines that process over 500K+ records daily, optimize complex SQL queries to improve dashboard refresh speeds by up to 30%, and design intuitive BI dashboards in Power BI and Tableau. I don’t just process data; I craft actionable insights that turn business challenges into growth opportunities.

Looking ahead, my goal is to deepen my expertise in machine learning and predictive analytics. I am driven to empower organizations to make forward-looking decisions that not only solve today's challenges but also create a measurable competitive advantage.
