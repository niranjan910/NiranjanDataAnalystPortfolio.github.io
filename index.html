Every Dataset Tells a Story — Here’s Mine.

I believe that inside every dataset lies a powerful narrative waiting to be told. My passion is to uncover that story and translate it into clear, strategic insights.

My journey into data began with a fascination for finding patterns in complexity. This curiosity led me to pursue a B.Tech in Computer Science, where I built a strong foundation in Python, SQL, and data visualization, discovering a passion for transforming raw numbers into compelling stories that drive strategic decisions.

In my current role as a Data Analyst Trainee at Naresh i Technologies, I specialize in developing end-to-end data solutions. I engineer robust ETL pipelines, optimize complex queries, and design intuitive dashboards in Power BI and Tableau. I don’t just process data; I craft actionable insights from it, turning vast datasets — like analyzing over 500,000 retail records — into clear business strategies.

Looking ahead, my goal is to deepen my expertise in machine learning and predictive analytics. I am driven to empower organizations to make forward-looking, data-informed decisions that not only solve today's challenges but also anticipate future opportunities and create a measurable competitive advantage.
