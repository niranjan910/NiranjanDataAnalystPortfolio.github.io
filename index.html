Every Dataset Tells a Story. Here’s Mine.

My journey from initial curiosity to professional data analysis.

My journey into data began with a simple spreadsheet in high school, analyzing student performance. That initial spark of curiosity led me to pursue a B.Tech in Computer Science (2019-2023), where I formally learned Python, SQL, and the art of data visualization. It was then I realized that data isn’t just numbers—it’s the language of insight.

As a Data Analyst Trainee at Naresh i Technologies, I now apply that passion professionally. I architect data pipelines, fine-tune SQL queries, and build dynamic dashboards in Power BI. Whether it’s sifting through 500K+ retail records or developing models for healthcare, my goal is always the same: transform raw data into a clear, compelling business story.
